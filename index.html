Software Engineer Intern
NextGen Justice | San Jose, CA

June 2025 – August 2025

I worked on the development of a full-stack, AI-powered legal assistance platform. This application integrates fine-tuned AI models with a modular Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) pipeline to deliver context-aware legal responses, source citations, and document-based reasoning.

On the backend, I built RESTful APIs with NestJS, PostgreSQL, and Swagger UI, including a complete user settings service following the Model-Controller-Service architecture. I enhanced RAG pipelines by building a dedicated prompt template service with LangChain, and created a TypeScript endpoint that generates and stores document embeddings with Ollama and pgvector for scalable semantic search. On the frontend, I designed user flows and wireframes by hand before translating them into Figma, directly shaping the application's user experience.

TypeScript NestJS Node.js React PostgreSQL LangChain Prisma Docker Figma
Robotics Engineering Intern
Purdue University | West Lafayette, IN

May 2024 – July 2024

I helped design a smart container system using embedded systems and ML algorithms to analyze eating habits.
